Facts about the Sea

The Sea covers over 70 percent of our planet. Did you know that the Sea is home to the worlds largest structure? Yup thats right! The worlds largest structure is the Great Barrier Reef off the coast of Australia

Most of our oxygen comes from the sea, which is why we must protect it as much as possible. Every year, millions of plastic is found in the sea that harms the marine life. Humans must be more cautious in what they are using and should try to minumize their waste so that we can help keep our sea clean.
